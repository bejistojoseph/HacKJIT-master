Health Technology Solutions

PS ID Problem Statement
HT01 AI-powered Telemedicine Platform: Develop an AI-driven telemedicine platform that connects patients with doctors remotely. The platform can offer virtual consultations, health monitoring, and follow-up scheduling.
HT03 Personalized Health Monitoring App: Build an app that tracks user health metrics (heart rate, activity levels, sleep patterns) and provides personalized recommendations based on AI analysis to improve overall health.
HT04 Virtual Health Assistant Chatbot: Design a chatbot that helps users with routine health inquiries, appointment booking, and prescription reminders. It should also assist in first-level diagnosis based on symptoms provided by users.
HT05 Mental Health Support Application: Develop an app that provides mental health resources, mood tracking, and access to virtual counseling sessions. AI can be used to provide personalized mental health exercises and tips.
HT13 AI-based Symptom Checker: Design an AI-powered symptom checker that users can interact with by entering their symptoms, receiving possible diagnoses, and advice on whether to seek medical attention.
